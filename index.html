How to use
Quick reference
✕
▶
Studying
Each card shows the definition (EN + VI) and a Tình huống workplace scenario. Recall the term, then tap Reveal term. After reveal, read the Exam tips for exam keywords. Tap Still learning to hide the answer and re-study the same card, or Got it to move on.
▼
Filter
Tap the filter bar below the title to choose what to study — all cards, one of the four clusters (Competencies, People, Organization, Workplace), or a single domain. Switching filters restores that filter’s saved progress (or starts a new deck if none).
↻
Reshuffle
Tap the shuffle icon to re-randomize only the remaining cards. Completed cards stay completed.
✓
Progress & resume
Progress is saved automatically in this browser (localStorage) — reopen the page anytime to continue. Each filter (all / cluster / domain) keeps its own progress. The pills show remaining, completed, and total in the current filter.
↩
Still learning
Hides the term and tip, scrolls back to the top, and keeps you on the same card so you can try again. Does not skip to the next card — only Got it advances.
↺
Reset
Tap the reset icon (top right) to clear progress for the current filter and study those cards from the beginning. This does not erase progress in other filters.
☼
Theme
Tap the sun/moon icon to switch between dark and light mode. Your choice is saved in this browser.
❖
Quiz
Switch to Quiz to practice SHRM-style multiple choice: read the definition, then pick the correct term from 4 options. Wrong choices are preferably drawn from the same domain. Needs at least 4 cards in the current filter. Score is for the current session only.
☑
Hide U.S. Employment Law
Top-right US button (default off = hidden). Hides all U.S. Employment Law & Regulations cards from Flashcards and Quiz for non-US learners. Tap to show/hide. Preference is saved in this browser.
