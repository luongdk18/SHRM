How to use
Quick reference
✕
▶
Studying
Each card shows a definition. Try to recall the term, then tap Reveal term to check yourself. Mark Still learning to see the card again later, or Got it to move it to completed.
▼
Filter
Tap the filter bar below the title to choose what to study — all 391 cards, one of the four clusters (Competencies, People, Organization, Workplace), or a single domain. Cards are always shuffled when you switch.
↻
Reshuffle
Tap the shuffle icon (top right) to re-randomize the current deck at any time without changing your filter or resetting your progress.
✓
Progress
The pills at the top show cards remaining, completed, and the total in your current filter. The progress bar fills as you work through the deck. When you finish, tap Study again to restart.
↩
Still learning
Cards marked Still learning are shuffled back into the remaining deck at a random position — not sent to the end — so they come back around naturally.
